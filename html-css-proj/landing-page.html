Welcome!

This test HTML page includes data visualizations made in my group's July 2018 project.

The purpose of this project was to use Pandas and Matplotlib to data mine any subject of our choosing.
Our group decided to use data found from CDC's Annual Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System (BRFSS) Survey.
We focused on producing interesting-looking charts with Matplotlib that reflected Obesity rates against different factors available to us from the survey.
Click a link below to discover more about the different factors we compared to and produced charts for.
