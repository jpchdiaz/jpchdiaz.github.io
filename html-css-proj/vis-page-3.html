Overweight % vs. Income

About this chart

A horizontal bar chart showing the percent of respondents who were categorized as 'Overweight',
which is a separate category from 'obese.' This demonstrates the range of data we had to work with,
though we only had a week to use our newly-learned data-munging skills to go through such a massive
government dataset over the course of 5 years.

There are definitely many types of charts we could divide our dataset into, and it wasn't just limited to
making visualizations of respondents who only specifically were obese.
